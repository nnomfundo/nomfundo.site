About Me

Hi, I’m Nomfundo Mkhwanazi — an Aeronautical Engineering student at the University of the Witwatersrand. I’m fascinated by how things work — from aircraft and machines to the systems behind them. Creativity and problem-solving are at the heart of how I think, which naturally led me to explore code, CAD, and hands-on projects that turn theory into something you can see, test, and improve.
